Sunday · June 14

Poached Salmon & Broccoli Sprouts

1
EPA / DHA Sulforaphane

Ingredients

  • 6 oz wild salmon fillet (skin-on)
  • 1 cup broccoli sprouts
  • ½ lemon, sliced
  • 2 cups water or light broth
  • 3–4 peppercorns
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Sea salt

Preparation

  1. Bring water or broth to a bare simmer with aromatics.
  2. Poach salmon 8–10 minutes at 170–180°F.
  3. Rest briefly.
  4. Top with raw sprouts and lemon.

Monday · June 15

Avocado, Cucumber & Herb Bowl

2
Vitamin E Carotenoids Apigenin Silica

Ingredients

  • 1 avocado, cubed
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • Parsley, basil, mint
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt & pepper

Preparation

  1. Combine avocado and cucumber.
  2. Tear herbs over the bowl.
  3. Add lemon, salt, and pepper.

Tuesday · June 16

Kiwi, Chia & Hemp Yogurt Bowl

3
Vitamin C ALA Omega‑3 Fiber Low‑BCAA

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup hemp yogurt
  • 2 kiwis, sliced
  • Chia seeds
  • Hemp hearts
  • Cardamom

Preparation

  1. Add yogurt to a bowl.
  2. Sprinkle chia and rest briefly.
  3. Add kiwi, hemp hearts, cardamom.

Electrolyte‑Supported Fasting Day

4
Sodium Potassium Magnesium Autophagy‑supportive

Electrolyte protocol

  • Water with sea salt
  • Mineral water
  • Herbal tea
  • Optional magnesium

Guidelines

  1. Hydrate with electrolytes.
  2. Use mineral water for potassium.
  3. Use magnesium later in the day.

  5. Break fast gently the next day.
