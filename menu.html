Sunday · June 14

Poached Salmon &
Broccoli Sprouts

1
EPA / DHA Sulforaphane

Ingredients

  • 6 oz wild salmon fillet (skin-on for fat)
  • 1 cup fresh broccoli sprouts
  • ½ lemon, sliced
  • 2 cups water or light vegetable broth
  • 3–4 whole peppercorns
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Pinch of sea salt

Preparation

  1. Bring water or broth to a bare simmer (170–180°F) in a shallow pan with lemon, peppercorns, bay leaf, and salt.
  2. Slide salmon in skin-side down. Poach 8–10 min until flesh flakes easily — do not boil.
  3. Lift salmon out gently and rest 2 min on a plate.
  4. Top with raw broccoli sprouts and a squeeze of fresh lemon. Serve immediately.

Monday · June 15

Avocado, Cucumber
& Herb Bowl

2
Vitamin E Carotenoids Apigenin Silica
Zero cooking required

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe avocado, cubed
  • 1 medium cucumber, sliced
  • ¼ cup fresh flat-leaf parsley
  • ¼ cup fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tbsp fresh mint
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Flaky sea salt & black pepper

Preparation

  1. Cube avocado and slice cucumber into half-moons. Place in a wide bowl.
  2. Roughly tear parsley, basil, and mint directly over the bowl — tearing releases more polyphenols than chopping.
  3. Squeeze lemon juice over everything and toss gently without mashing the avocado.
  4. Season with flaky salt and cracked pepper. Eat immediately to preserve vitamin E.

Tuesday · June 16

Kiwi, Chia &
Hemp Yogurt Bowl

3
Vitamin C ALA Omega-3 Fiber Low-BCAA

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup plain hemp yogurt (unsweetened)
  • 2 ripe kiwis, peeled & sliced
  • 1½ tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 tbsp hemp hearts
  • 1 tsp raw honey (optional)
  • Pinch of ground cardamom

Preparation

  1. Spoon hemp yogurt into a bowl as the base.
  2. Sprinkle chia seeds over yogurt and let sit 3–5 min to begin softening.
  3. Arrange sliced kiwi on top. Scatter hemp hearts and a pinch of cardamom.
  4. Drizzle with honey if desired. Serve right away — kiwi's vitamin C degrades quickly once cut.

Wednesday · June 17

Egg-White, Spinach
& Mushroom Scramble

4
Ergothioneine Lutein Low-BCAA

Ingredients

  • 4 large egg whites
  • 1 cup shiitake or oyster mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 big handfuls baby spinach
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp avocado oil
  • Sea salt, black pepper, fresh thyme

Preparation

  1. Set pan to low-medium heat (≤250°F). High heat creates AGEs — keep it gentle.
  2. Add avocado oil, then garlic and mushrooms. Sauté 4–5 min until just tender.
  3. Add spinach and toss until wilted, about 1 min.
  4. Pour egg whites over vegetables. Fold slowly with a silicone spatula. Remove from heat when just set and still slightly glossy. Season with salt, pepper, and thyme.

Thursday · June 18

Collagen-Protein
Green Soup

5
Collagen peptides Low BCAA Antioxidant-preserved

Ingredients

  • 2 scoops unflavored collagen peptides
  • 1 cup green cabbage, roughly chopped
  • 1 medium zucchini, diced
  • 1 large leek, white & light-green parts, sliced
  • 2 stalks celery, chopped
  • 4 cups low-sodium vegetable broth
  • 1 bay leaf, sea salt, white pepper, fresh parsley

Preparation

  1. Bring broth to a gentle simmer — not a rolling boil — to preserve antioxidants in the greens.
  2. Add leeks, celery, and bay leaf. Simmer 5 min.
  3. Add cabbage and zucchini. Continue at low simmer 8–10 min until tender but still vibrant.
  4. Remove from heat. Stir in collagen peptides until dissolved — add off-heat to avoid denaturing. Season and top with fresh parsley.

Friday · June 19

Steamed Cod, Olive Oil
& Broccoli Sprouts

6
Oleocanthal Sulforaphane Low BCAA protein

Ingredients

  • 6 oz fresh cod fillet
  • 1 cup fresh broccoli sprouts
  • 1½ tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • ½ lemon
  • 2 sprigs fresh dill or thyme
  • Sea salt & white pepper

Preparation

  1. Set a steamer basket over 1–2 inches of simmering water. Season cod with salt and white pepper.
  2. Place cod in steamer with herb sprigs on top. Cover and steam 7–9 min until opaque and flaky.
  3. Transfer to a plate. Drizzle with olive oil while warm — applying to hot fish degrades oleocanthal.
  4. Top generously with raw broccoli sprouts and a squeeze of lemon. Serve at once.
